Definition:

An attribute that sets the tooltip (message that appears when you hover over the element) of the element

Usage:

<element title="some message"></element>

Examples:

<p title="Be sure to drink your Ovaltine!">I have a secret message. Hover over me to find out what it is!</p>

I have a secret message. Hover over me to find out what it is!

Notice that you can hover anywhere in the dashed box and still get the tooltip to show
