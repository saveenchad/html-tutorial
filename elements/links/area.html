Definition:

Allows defined sections of an image-map to be created

Usage:

<img src="some-image-map" usemap="#unique-map-name">
<map name="unique-map-name">
  <area alt="text" coords="shape coordinates" download="filename" href="URL" hreflang="language_code" media="media query" shape="default | rect | circle | poly">
  ...
</map>
