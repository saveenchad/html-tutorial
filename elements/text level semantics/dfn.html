Definition:

The <dfn> tag represents the defining instance of a term in HTML. The defining instance is often the first use of a term in a document. The nearest parent of the <dfn> tag must also contain the definition/explanation for the term inside <dfn>.

Examples:

Example 1:

Loquacious means tending to talk a great deal; talkative.
Computer Science is the study of the principles and use of computers.

Example 2:

We can also add the title attribute of a <dfn> tag:

UCSD is a university in San Diego.

Example 3:

We can also add the abbr tag inside the <dfn> tag:

UCSD is a university in San Diego.
